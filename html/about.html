The yujin experience
At Curry Kebab & Bar Yujin, we bring the flavors of South Asia to the heart of Japan. From sizzling kebabs and fluffy naan to handmade momo and rich, aromatic curries, our menu celebrates the food we grew up with — crafted with care, passion, and authenticity.

Our journey started with a simple idea: to create a place where people can enjoy home-style Nepalese and Indian cooking in a lively, welcoming atmosphere. Whether you’re here for a casual lunch, a family dinner, or late-night drinks with friends, we want you to feel at home.

We take pride in using fresh ingredients, bold spices, and traditional recipes, balanced with a modern touch that suits every taste. Pair your meal with our selection of beverages, and you’ll find that every visit has something new to enjoy.

Located in Japan, our café is more than just a restaurant — it’s a cultural exchange, a place where East meets South Asia, and where good food brings people together.

Come for the kebabs, stay for the momo, and leave with a smile.
